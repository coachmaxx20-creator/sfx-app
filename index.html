How To Trade
1️⃣
Get your signal
Tap "New Signal". The AI analyses RSI, EMA, MACD and ADX across multiple timeframes to give you a UP or DOWN direction with a confidence score.
2️⃣
Open Pocket Option
Open the Pocket Option app. Select the same asset shown in the signal (e.g. AUD/USD OTC). Set the trade time to 5 seconds.
3️⃣
Place your trade
Signal says UP → tap the green button on Pocket Option. Signal says DOWN → tap the red button. Place before the countdown hits zero.
⚠️
Risk Warning
Trading involves real financial risk. Never trade money you cannot afford to lose. Signals are based on technical analysis — no signal is 100% guaranteed.
